My Journal

Health monitoring and fall detection using IoT advantages can improve the daily lifestyle of elderly people. Our paper IoT Based Health Monitoring & Fall Detection System, present an IoT based health monitoring and fall detection system for elderly people. The proposed architecture presents both health monitoring and fall detecting on together. For health monitoring, the health parameters are transferred using RFID signal. Both the data for health monitoring and fall detection are then classified and analyzed. After that, the results are sent via Bluetooth, Server and GSM to the receiving device.
